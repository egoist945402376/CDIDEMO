Welcome to our comprehensive collection of regulations, tools, and resources for farmers looking to export agricultural products to international markets. These resources will help you understand and comply with key requirements necessary for successful market access.

EU Regulations

Food Safety Laws (General Food Law - EC 178/2002)

Ensuring product safety, traceability, and contamination prevention.
Pesticide Residue Standards (MRLs - EC 396/2005)

Setting pesticide residue limits; non-compliance leads to import bans.
Labeling & Traceability (EU 1169/2011)

Requiring full labeling, including product name, origin, ingredients, allergens, and nutrition facts.
Sustainability Requirements (EU Organic 2018/848)

Demanding organic certification for organic imports.
Sanitary & Phytosanitary Measures (SPS Agreement)

Requiring quarantine checks for fresh fruits, meat, and other sensitive products.
Common Agricultural Policy (CAP)

EU's agricultural policy framework that affects market access requirements.

Dutch Customs Assistance

Information on importing goods to the Netherlands through Dutch Customs.

Online Training Resources

BTSF and BTSF ACADEMY

Better Training for Safer Food (BTSF) is a European Commission training initiative to improve the knowledge and implementation of EU rules covering food safety, plant, animal, and One Health.

The courses address the following topics:

  • Plant Health
  • Pesticides
  • Food Safety
  • Organic Farming
